holder tree top holders, connected by where tokens came from

Opens a full-screen family-tree map — the bonding curve sits at the top, with your top holders branching off it, connected to whichever source the data actually traced (the curve itself, or another top holder). Every wallet appears exactly once, no matter how many branches connect to it. Click any box to trace that wallet's own recent transfers and expand its children directly underneath it — each one gets checked against its current balance, so a wallet that received tokens but has since sold or moved them all on shows up clearly marked "SOLD" instead of looking like an active holder. Clicking any box also opens a side panel with its estimated gain/loss. Traces every top-level holder's origin up front, so it can take a little while to open — real RPC work, not a full history since launch (that would mean scanning far more transactions than any free tier allows).
